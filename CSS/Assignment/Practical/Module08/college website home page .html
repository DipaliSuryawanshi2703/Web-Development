About

Shivaji University, established in 1962, is a state university located at Kolhapur, Maharashtra, India. The university, with a campus spread over 853 acres, is named in honour of Chatrapati Shivaji. It was inaugurated on 18 November 1962 by Dr. Radhakrishnan, the then president of India.
Address: Swarbatwadi Toll Plaza, Campus, Vidya Nagar, Kolhapur, Maharashtra 416004
Students: 300,000 (2020)
Chancellor: Governor of Maharashtra
Founded: 18 November 1962
Campus: Urban
School type: Public university
Motto: (ज्ञानमेवामृतम्)
Vice-Chancellor: Digambar Tukaram Shirke
